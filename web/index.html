UTA4: Medical Imaging DICOM Files Dataset

In this dataset, we present the medical images of patients diagnosed by clinicians during our User Tests and Analysis 4 (UTA4) study.

  • Discover

About

The dataset is part of the work done by SIPg, an ISR-Lisboa research group, and ITI, two R&D Units of LARSyS. The project also involves the collaborative effort of INESC-ID. Both ISR-Lisboa and INESC-ID are Associate Laboratories of IST from ULisboa. This dataset will be used in BreastScreening, MIDA and MIMBCD-UI projects.

Description

Dataset Details

The UTA4: Medical Imaging DICOM Files Dataset consists of a study providing several medical images of patients on the DICOM format diagnosed by clinicians. The study was performed with 31 clinicians from several clinical institutions in Portugal. The number of participants and respective institutions are: (1) 8 clinicians from Hospital Fernando Fonseca; (2) 12 clinicians from IPO-Lisboa; (3) 1 clinician from Hospital de Santa Maria; (4) 8 clinicians from IPO-Coimbra; (5) 1 clinician from Madeira Medical Center; and (6) 1 clinician from SAMS. Clinicians' experience ranged from 5 to 30 years of medical practice. Recruited specialists are in advanced career positions. Participants were observed and interviewed in a semi-structured fashion.
